OUR STORY

Kings Hotel is a family-owned and operated hotel based in Mombasa, Kenya, with branches in Nyali and Zanzibar. The hotel was founded in 1960 by John and Mary King, who had a vision of creating a luxurious and welcoming oasis for travelers from all over the world.

Over the years, Kings Hotel has grown and evolved, but it has always remained true to its family roots. The King family is passionate about hospitality, and they are committed to providing their guests with the best possible experience.

The Kings Hotel team is made up of dedicated and experienced professionals, many of whom have been with the company for many years. The team is committed to providing guests with warm and friendly service, and they go out of their way to make sure that every guest feels at home.

Kings Hotel is a place where families and friends can come together to create lasting memories. The hotel offers a variety of amenities and activities to keep guests entertained, including private beaches, swimming pools, spas, fitness centers, restaurants, and bars. Kings Hotel also offers a variety of tours and excursions, so guests can explore all that Mombasa, Nyali, and Zanzibar have to offer.

The Kings family is grateful for the support of their guests over the years, and they look forward to continuing to provide them with the best possible hospitality experience for many years to come.

OUR PRINCIPLES

  • Ensure clients that we are here to take care of them from the moment they arrive.
